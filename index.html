BULACAN

Bulacan is a province in the Philippines, located in the Central Luzon Region in the island of Luzon.

WHY CHOOSE BULACAN?

Historical

 - is established at 1578, which made it full of historical places.
 - many national heroes and political figures were born in Bulacan 
 - the province was also one of the first to revolt against Spain

 Environment

- has a green environment that makes the air fresh and gives a             relaxing environment
 - has many beautiful tourist destinations 
 - also have the welcoming people of Bulacan

 Location

 - is located just north of Metro Manila and shares boundaries with     Pampanga and Nueva Ecija in the north, and Manila Bay on the     south west.  
 - have several restaurants all over the place
